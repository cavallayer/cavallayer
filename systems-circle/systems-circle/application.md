# Systems Circle — Membership Application

Minimal. Architectural. Signal over noise.

Read the [charter](charter.md) and [norms](norms.md) before applying.

**To apply:** Copy all questions below, write your answers, and email to [securtright@gmail.com](mailto:securtright@gmail.com) with subject line: **Systems Circle Application — [Your Name]**

There are no word limits. There is no right answer. There is no wrong format. Answer as you think, not as you think you should think.

---

## Section 0 — Contact

1. Name
2. Email
3. LinkedIn (optional)
4. Website / GitHub / Portfolio (optional)

---

## Section 1 — Identity

5. What system do you build, govern, or operate?
6. What is the core problem your system exists to solve?
7. What is the highest-stakes decision your system makes or influences?
8. What is the most important constraint in your system?
9. What is the clearest signal that your system is failing?

---

## Section 2 — Architecture & Reasoning

10. Describe a failure mode in your system and how you identified it.
11. Describe a decision you made where clarity mattered more than consensus.
12. Describe a time you imposed structure on ambiguity.

---

## Section 3 — Operating Style

13. How do you prefer to receive challenge?
14. What does accountability look like in your work?
15. What do you do when something ambiguous lands on your plate?

---

## Section 4 — Fit

16. What do you want from a high-signal systems circle?
17. What do you not want?
18. What would make this circle a waste of your time?

---

## Section 5 — Moral Orientation

19. What governs your decisions when no rule requires it and no one is watching?
20. What is your plumb line?

---

## Section 6 — System Representation

21. Represent your system. Format is yours. Diagram, outline, schema, or prose. Hand-drawn and photographed is fine. Attach to your email.
