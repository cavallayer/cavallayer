# Systems Circle — Norms

These are not rules. They are the operating conditions that make this circle worth being in.

---

## Clarity over comfort

Say the clear thing, not the comfortable thing.
If something is wrong, name it.
If something is failing, say so.
Ambiguity is not kindness here.

---

## Structure over storytelling

Bring a model, a framework, a diagram, a constraint.
Do not bring a narrative looking for an audience.
We are here to think together, not to witness each other.

---

## Rigor over vibes

Good intentions are not good governance.
Enthusiasm is not a plan.
A system is not well-designed because you care about it.
Bring evidence, logic, and failure modes — not passion.

---

## Responsibility over ego

You are accountable for your system.
Not your team. Not the environment. Not the circumstances.
Bring what is yours to own.

---

## Directness over politics

Say what you mean.
Challenge what needs challenging.
