# Systems Circle — 55-Minute Session Structure

No fluff. No emotional processing. No storytelling.
Structure, clarity, rigor, and plumb line.

Room opens at the hour. We end at 55 minutes regardless of where the conversation is.

---

## 0–3 minutes — Buffer

Room is open. People settle. No formal content. Late arrivals welcome.

*Tone: silent, ambient. No small talk required.*

---

## 3–8 minutes — Frame

- State the purpose of the session
- Reaffirm norms
- Set expectations for the hour

*Tone: concise, direct, high-signal. No welcome speeches.*
